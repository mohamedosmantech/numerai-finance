Numerai Finance - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

Summary: Numerai Finance is a financial intelligence tool. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All calculations are performed in real-time and discarded immediately.

1. Information We Collect

Numerai Finance collects only the calculation inputs you provide:

  • Loan Calculator: Principal amount, interest rate, loan term
  • Investment Calculator: Initial investment, return rate, time period, contribution amounts
  • Tax Estimator: Income amount, filing status, deductions, state

We do NOT collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Account credentials or passwords
  • Social Security numbers or government IDs
  • Bank account or credit card numbers
  • Location data or IP addresses
  • Device identifiers or cookies

2. How We Use Information

Calculation inputs are used solely to:

  • Perform the requested financial calculation
  • Return results to you immediately

Data is processed in real-time and not stored after the calculation is complete.

3. Data Sharing

We do NOT share any data with third parties. Period.

  • No advertising partners
  • No analytics services
  • No data brokers
  • No affiliates

4. Data Retention

We do not retain any user data. Calculation inputs exist only during the processing of your request and are immediately discarded.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Numerai Finance does NOT use:

  • Cookies
  • Tracking pixels
  • Analytics scripts
  • Fingerprinting

6. Children's Privacy

Numerai Finance is suitable for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Security

All communications use HTTPS encryption. Since we don't store data, there is no database that could be breached.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy:
